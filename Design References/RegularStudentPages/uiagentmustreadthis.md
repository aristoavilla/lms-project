Regular Student Pages

These are the few things that you should update or add into each pages for Regular Student role.

1. Dashboard
    - If you open one of the card, you will be redirected to a page of the card. Example, you are opening the Pending Submission, you will be redirected to assignments page. 

2. Assignments
    The page will only have 4 navigations (replace the current navigations in the project):
    1) On-task/Upcoming/Need to be worked on (pick a good word for this page that the student needs to work on): Assignment items are ordered from the most near deadline to the least near deadline.
    2) Pending: This means you have submitted your assignment and is waiting for being graded by the teacher
    3) Graded: Your graded assignments end here. You can see the score of your assignment and then you can see the comments given by the teacher.
    4) Archived: This is for the assignments that have been graded and have been seen by the student (opened). Studetns could review their answers and materials here.

    - When you open an assignment item, you can see the details of the assignments like the answers you have submitted, file, quiz, etc. You can see who is the teacher that posted this assignment, deadline, the description/instructions given by the teacher either with texts or with file.
    - You can't see the others' grades or any progress of the others.
